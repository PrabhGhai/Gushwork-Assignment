Frequently Asked Questions

What is the expected lifespan of Mangalam HDPE pipes?
Mangalam HDPE pipes are engineered for a service life of over 50 years when operated within specified temperature and pressure parameters.
Are these pipes suitable for drinking water transportation?
Yes, our pipes are BIS certified and manufactured from food-grade virgin PE100 material, making them perfectly safe for potable water.
How do HDPE pipes handle extreme weather conditions?
HDPE is highly resistant to UV radiation and can withstand temperatures from -40°C to +80°C without becoming brittle or losing structural integrity.
Can these pipes be used in chemical processing plants?
Absolutely. HDPE has excellent chemical resistance to most corrosive acids and alkalis, making it an industry standard for chemical waste.
